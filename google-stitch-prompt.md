## PROMPT (paste directly into Google Stitch)

### Design Style Directives

- **Mood:** Cinematic, premium, forward-moving. Think luxury with edge — not soft or pastel.
- **Spacing:** Generous padding, breathing room. Large section gutters.
- **Layout:** Mostly left-aligned for hierarchy. Occasional centered moments for impact (Coming Soon, closing statements).
- **Borders & Accents:** Use 3px terracotta left-border bars, bottom-border hover effects, and thin horizontal rules as design elements throughout.
- **Hover States:** Subtle background shifts, terracotta border reveals, slight upward transforms on buttons.
- **Animations:** Fade-up reveal on scroll for content sections. Smooth CTA hover transitions. Countdown timer. Marquee scroll.
- **No gradients on backgrounds** — keep backgrounds flat dark navy. Use terracotta for the one focal radial glow in the hero only.
- **No rounded corners** except pill badges and form inputs. Cards and buttons should be sharp (border-radius: 3px max).
- **Do not use stock-photo hero backgrounds.** Keep it graphic and typographic.

---

### Tone
Bold. Precise. Confident. Elevated. Every word and visual element should signal that this brand delivers exceptional, personalised experiences for discerning travelers.
